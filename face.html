Profile

Name: Ivan Fyodorovich Karamazov
Nickname:Vanka
Nationality: Russian
Occupation:Recently graduated student

Ivan is the middle son of Fyodor and the first from the wife his father loved. He is lofty, intelligent, firm, and cold at appearance which hides his passion, love, uncertainty, and suffering. When the novel concludes, Ivan had not yet reconciled his dichotomy, however, Dostoevsky intended this to be the first book of a trilogy. Unfortunately, he died before his aim was realized and so in a reader's speculation, I believe Ivan would have triumphed over his struggle and yielded his reason to the appearance of God. For he who seeks will find.
